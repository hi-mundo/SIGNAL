## Community commitment

SIGNAL is intended to be shared infrastructure for people designing better user experiences in LLM-based systems.

By using or adapting SIGNAL, you are encouraged to follow this community commitment:

- Preserve attribution to the original framework.
- Make meaningful improvements visible to others when possible.
- Share derivative framework materials under the same open terms.
- Clearly document changes, additions, removals, and translations.
- Avoid presenting modified versions as the official SIGNAL framework unless they are accepted by the maintainers.
- Avoid implying endorsement by SIGNAL, its creator, or its contributors without explicit permission.
- Contribute improvements back through issues, pull requests, discussions, examples, critiques, or references when possible.
